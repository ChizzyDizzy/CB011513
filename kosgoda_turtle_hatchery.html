About Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is a well-known turtle conservation project situated in the village of Kosgoda, on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. It is dedicated to protecting and conserving sea turtles, which are endangered species.

Turtle Conservation

The primary objective of the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is to safeguard sea turtles and their eggs. The hatchery collects eggs from vulnerable nesting sites along the coastline and provides a protected environment for incubation. This increases the chances of survival for the hatchlings and contributes to the conservation efforts of these magnificent creatures.

Guided Tour

Visitors to the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery can take guided tours to gain insights into the lives of different turtle species found in Sri Lanka. Knowledgeable staff members provide informative explanations about the turtle's life cycle, nesting habits, and the conservation challenges they face. You can learn fascinating facts about the Green Turtle, Olive Ridley Turtle, Loggerhead Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, and Leatherback Turtle.

Hatchling Release

One of the most exciting activities at the hatchery is the opportunity to participate in the release of baby turtles into the ocean. Visitors can actively engage in this conservation practice by helping the tiny turtles make their way to the sea. It is a memorable and rewarding experience to witness the hatchlings embark on their journey to the water.

Education and Awareness

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery is dedicated to educating the public about the importance of turtle conservation. They organize educational programs, presentations, and workshops for visitors of all ages. By raising awareness about the threats faced by sea turtles, the hatchery aims to promote responsible tourism and sustainable practices to ensure their long-term survival.

Rehabilitation Center

In addition to protecting turtle eggs, the hatchery serves as a rehabilitation center for injured or sick turtles. They provide necessary medical attention, nursing care, and rehabilitation programs to help these turtles recover. Once the turtles have regained their health, they are released back into the ocean.

Souvenir Shop

The hatchery often has a souvenir shop where visitors can purchase turtle-themed merchandise, including handcrafted items, artwork, and clothing. The proceeds from these sales contribute to the funding of the hatchery's conservation efforts.

Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Visiting the Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery offers an incredible opportunity to learn about sea turtles, actively participate in their conservation, and witness the positive impact of conservation efforts. It is a rewarding experience that appeals to nature lovers, families, and anyone interested in marine conservation.
