About Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

The Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is a popular tourist attraction located in the town of Hikkaduwa, on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. It is a conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles, which are an endangered species.

Turtle Conservation

The main focus of the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is to protect sea turtles and their eggs. The hatchery collects turtle eggs from vulnerable nesting sites along the coast and keeps them in a protected area until they hatch. This helps to increase the survival rate of the baby turtles and contribute to the conservation efforts.

Turtle Hatchery Tour

Visitors to the hatchery can take guided tours to learn about the different turtle species found in Sri Lanka, such as the Green Turtle, Loggerhead Turtle, Hawksbill Turtle, Olive Ridley Turtle, and Leatherback Turtle. The knowledgeable staff at the hatchery provide interesting information about the life cycle, habits, and conservation challenges faced by these turtles.

Baby Turtle Release

One of the highlights of visiting the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery is the opportunity to release baby turtles into the ocean. This hands-on experience allows visitors to actively participate in the conservation efforts by helping these little creatures reach their natural habitat.

Education and Awareness

The hatchery plays a vital role in educating the public about the importance of turtle conservation. They organize informative presentations, workshops, and awareness programs for visitors of all ages. By raising awareness about the threats faced by sea turtles, the hatchery aims to promote responsible tourism and sustainable practices.

Rehabilitation of Injured Turtles

The hatchery also serves as a rehabilitation center for injured and sick turtles. They provide medical assistance and care to injured turtles, helping them recover before releasing them back into the ocean.

Souvenir Shop

The hatchery usually has a small souvenir shop where visitors can purchase turtle-themed merchandise, such as t-shirts, handicrafts, and artwork. The proceeds from these sales often go towards supporting the conservation efforts of the hatchery.

Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Visiting the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery provides a unique opportunity to witness the conservation efforts firsthand, learn about the importance of protecting sea turtles, and actively contribute to their survival. It is an enriching and educational experience for nature enthusiasts, families, and anyone interested in marine conservation.
